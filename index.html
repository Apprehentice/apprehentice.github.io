Home

Hey all, I'm Apprehentice, a game developer born and raised in Los Angeles! This website is my stream of consciousness distilled into text and images for your viewing [dis]pleasure! Join me on my journey to make the next Mario World or Zelda or something with all the joys and frustrations that come along with it!

Reading this blog, you'll find that I like to hide stuff in the articles. Since this blog is a sort of stream of consciousness, I've decided that things posted here should stay here unless it's somehow harmful to leave it here. Consider any transgressions or imbalances documentation of my personal development and character building. After all, we never stop learning.

If blogs aren't your thing, subscribe to me on YouTube or follow me on Mixer. I do live coding there and I'd love some company. God knows I can't focus on my work, otherwise.
