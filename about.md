This blog is where I go to express my thoughts and ideas to the world. Perhaps my experience could be useful to somebody. Either way, it's probably go to get it out there, if only so you can see my point of view.

This blog serves as a timeline for my thoughts, ideas, and past learning experiences. People talk about the right and wrong ways to do things, but they don't really show how they got to that conclusion. Perhaps my ramblings can shed some light on that subject. Feel free to read around here or follow me on Twitter [(@foxtrot200)](https://twitter.com/foxtrot200). Hell, come talk to me if you're so inclined!
